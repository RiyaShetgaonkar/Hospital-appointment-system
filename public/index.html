Welcome to Care Connect

This website is a real-time hospital and clinic appointment management system designed to replace traditional physical queues with a digital token-based queue. Patients can book appointments online, receive a token, track their live position in the queue, view estimated waiting time, and cancel if needed. Doctors can monitor the live queue, call the next patient, view patient details and medical history, and manage the flow efficiently. The system uses real-time updates to ensure both patients and doctors always see the latest queue status, reducing waiting time, crowding, and improving overall clinic efficiency.

Key Features

Online Booking

Patients can easily book appointments online and receive a unique digital token instantly.

Live Queue Tracking

Track your position in the queue in real-time and view estimated waiting times.

Doctor Dashboard

Doctors can monitor the queue, call patients, and access medical histories seamlessly.

Real-Time Updates

Instant notifications and updates ensure everyone stays informed without delays.

Easy Cancellation

Cancel appointments effortlessly if plans change, freeing up slots for others.

Improved Efficiency

Reduce crowding and waiting times, making clinics more efficient and patient-friendly.
